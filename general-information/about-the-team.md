Needham High School is a member of the [Bay State Conference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bay_State_Conference) comprising of 10 high schools in two divisions, divided by geography: the Carey Division (Brookline, Framingham, Natick, Newton North, and Wellesley) and the Herget Division (Braintree, Milton, Needham, Walpole, and Weymouth).
