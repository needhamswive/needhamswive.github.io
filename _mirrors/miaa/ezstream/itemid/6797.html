--- title: 2012 Boys State Division 1 Swim Psych Sheet date: 2012-02-17 --- PARKING INFORMATION: All spectators will need to park across the street in the Business school lot for the duration of the meet. We have no agreement with the shopping center, but it is a large shopping center so the bus can park there with the driver in place for the duration of the meet.
